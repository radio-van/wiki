# myths

## sharks kill humans
Every year sharks kill ~40-50 humans (number of attacks is ~1000).  
By contrast, snakes kill 80-100 thousands humans.

## how to escape shark
Pretending as dead is useless, shark can sense electricity of living  
bodies and eats scavangers.
Repellents are useless.

## supersharks
Size of Megaladons was maximum 15m, because of *cube-square law*,  
gills can't provide enough oxygen to such huge body.

## sharks can't breathe if not moving
Sharks can stand still, because they have alternative breathing  
mechanism (not all of them).
