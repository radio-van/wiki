# ingridients
* chicken/turkey 600g
* potatoes 10p
* carrot 1-2p
* onion 1p
* rice/beans

# howto
- [optional] brew beans for 1h (or add them at the next step)
- water + meat - until boiling, then 1:30 **4.5**
- add onion
- potatoes + carrot - fry 10 min **5**
- p+c add 30 min before the end
- rice add 15 min before the end
- remove onion
- salt
