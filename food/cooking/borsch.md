# ingridients
* water 2l
* pork/beef 400g
* beets 2p
* carrot 1p
* onion 3p
* tomato paste 2 dinner spoon
* oil 4-5 dinner spoon
* lemon acid 1 pinch
* cabbage 300g
* potatoes 4p

# howto
- water + meat - until boiling, then 1-1.5 hours under cover, **3**
- onion + carrot - fry 5 min
- + beets + lemon acid - fry 5 min
- stir, fry 5-7 min
- cut meat
- add cabbage to broth - 10 min
- + potatoes - until ready (soft)
- + meat + roast - 5-7 min
- + salt
